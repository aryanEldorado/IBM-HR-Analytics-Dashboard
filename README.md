# 👥 IBM HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze IBM HR Analytics employee data. The dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, workforce demographics, department performance, salary distribution, job satisfaction, and overtime trends to support HR decision-making.

## 🎯 Features
- 👨‍💼 Total Employees KPI
- 🎂 Average Age KPI
- 💰 Average Monthly Income KPI
- 📉 Attrition Rate KPI
- 🏢 Employees by Department
- 🚪 Attrition by Department
- 😊 Job Satisfaction by Job Role
- 💵 Average Monthly Income by Job Role
- 🎓 Monthly Income by Education Field
- ⏰ Overtime vs Attrition
- 👨‍🦰 Gender Distribution

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- IBM HR Analytics Dataset (CSV)
- Microsoft Excel

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Employees: **1,470**
- Average Age: **36.92 Years**
- Average Monthly Income: **6,503**
- Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
- Research & Development has the largest workforce.
- Sales department shows significant employee attrition.
- Managers have the highest average monthly income.
- Employees working overtime experience higher attrition.
